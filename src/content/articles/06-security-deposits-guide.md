### Legal Limits

**There is NO legal maximum** for security deposits in Israel, but common practice is:
- **Residential:** 1-3 months' rent
- **Commercial:** 3-6 months' rent
- **Luxury/furnished:** Up to 6 months' rent

Excessive deposits may be challenged in court as unreasonable.
