Tracking your rental income and expenses is a core part of property management. RentMate simplifies this process and provides powerful analytics to help you understand your financial performance.

## The Payments Hub

The Payments page is your centralized view for all financial transactions across your portfolio.

Here you can:
- View a timeline of all recorded transactions.
- See the status of each payment (Paid, Pending, Overdue).
- Filter transactions by property, type (income vs. expense), or date.

> [!NOTE]  
> The summary cards at the top of the Payments page give you a quick snapshot of your monthly and annual cash flow.

![RentMate Payments Page](/images/support/payments-page.png)

## Logging a New Payment or Expense

Keeping your records up-to-date is easy. Instead of managing complex spreadsheets, you can log individual transactions directly from the Payments tab or within a specific property's hub.

1. Navigate to the **Payments** app section or select a Property.
2. Click the **➕ Add Payment** (הוסף תשלום) or **Log Expense** button.
3. Fill in the transaction details including Amount, Date, Asset, and whether it's an Income (rent) or Expense (repairs, taxes).
4. Save the transaction.

![Log Payment Modal](/images/support/log-payment-modal.png)

## Analyzing Your Portfolio

Once you have established a history of payments and expenses, RentMate unlocks a dynamic visual suite to help you measure profitability.

Navigate to the **Analytics** page to access:
- **Net Yield:** See your actual return on investment across properties.
- **Income vs. Expense Charts:** Compare your revenue alongside your combined costs visually over the year.
- **Occupancy Rates:** Track historical vacancy periods.
