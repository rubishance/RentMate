### Dispute Resolution Hierarchy

**Level 1: Direct Communication**
- Discuss issue calmly
- Email/text for documentation
- Seek mutual solution
- **Success rate:** 60-70%

**Level 2: Written Formal Notice**
- State issue clearly
- Reference contract clause
- Propose solution with deadline
- Send via registered mail
- **Success rate:** 20-25%

**Level 3: Mediation**
- Neutral third party
- Faster and cheaper than court
- Non-binding (usually)
- Preserves relationship
- **Success rate:** 50-60%

**Level 4: Small Claims Court**
- For disputes under â‚ª35,000
- No lawyer required
- 2-6 month timeline
- Binding decision
- **Success rate:** Varies

**Level 5: Magistrate Court**
- For larger disputes
- Lawyer recommended
- 6-18 month timeline
- Formal legal process
- **Success rate:** Varies
