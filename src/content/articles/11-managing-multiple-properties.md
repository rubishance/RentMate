### Time-Saving Strategies

- Schedule all property inspections same week
- Pay all bills on same day
- Review all maintenance requests at once
- Process rent payments together

#### 2. **Create Templates**
- Lease agreements
- Move-in/move-out checklists
- Maintenance request forms
- Rent increase notices
- Contract renewal letters

#### 3. **Delegate When Possible**
- Hire property manager for 5-10% of rent
- Use virtual assistant for admin tasks

- Automatic rent collection via bank transfer
- Scheduled CPI adjustments

- Can manage personally
- Part-time effort

- Hire property manager OR
- Full-time self-management
- Professional systems required

### Financial Organization

**Account structure:**
- One business checking account
- Separate savings for reserves (6 months expenses)
- Credit card for property expenses (points!)
- Clear separation from personal finances

**Reserve fund:**
- Target: 6-12 months operating expenses
- Covers: Vacancies, major repairs, legal costs
- Build: 10% of monthly rent into reserves

### Legal Compliance at Scale

**Challenges:**
- Multiple lease expiration dates
- Different CPI adjustment dates
- Varying local regulations
- Tax complexity

**Solutions:**
- Calendar alerts for all deadlines
- Standardize lease terms where possible
- Annual legal review
- Professional tax preparation
