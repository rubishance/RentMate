### Madad (××“×“ - CPI / Consumer Price Index)
An index published by the Central Bureau of Statistics on the 15th of every month. It measures inflation (the cost of living).
*   **Linkage to Madad:** Many long-term contracts link the rent to this index. If the Madad rises by 1%, the rent rises by 1%.
*   **Base Index:** The value of the Madad on the day the contract was signed. All future calculations are compared to this number.

### 10% Tax Track (××¡×œ×•×œ 10%)
A simplified tax route for residential landlords. You pay a flat **10% tax** on your gross rental income.
*   **Pros:** Very simple, no accountant needed usually.
*   **Cons:** You cannot deduct expenses (repairs, mortgage interest) from the taxable amount.
