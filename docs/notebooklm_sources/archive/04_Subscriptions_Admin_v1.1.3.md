# RentMate: Subscriptions & Admin Operations (v1.1.2)

RentMate uses a tier-based model to accommodate single-unit landlords and professional property management firms.

## 1. Subscription Tiers

### Free Forever
- **Capacity**: 1 Property.
- **Features**: Basic contract tracking, standard rent calculator, and basic support chat.
- **Limits**: AI Analysis and Advanced Ledger calculations are restricted.

### Pro (Landlord)
- **Capacity**: Up to 5 Properties (Standard) / 10 Properties (Pro Max).
- **Features**: Full AI Contract Analysis, Unlimited Document Storage, and Advanced Ledger/Index calculations.

### Business (Manager)
- **Capacity**: Unlimited Properties.
- **Features**: Team access, white-labeled invoices, and priority support.

---

## 2. Professional Admin Center
Administrators (role: 'admin') have access to systemic tools to oversee the platform:

### User & Support Management
- **User Dashboard**: Admins can view user profiles, check their account status, and modify plan levels (e.g., manual upgrades).
- **Impersonation**: Subject to privacy rules, admins can assist users by viewing their dashboard (support mode).

### System Health
- **Maintenance Mode**: Admins can toggle the site's maintenance state.
- **AI Analytics**: Monitoring tool for AI token consumption and message frequency to manage system costs.
- **Broadcast System**: Capabilities to send professional mass updates about app changes or Israeli legal updates.

---

## 3. Scaling a Portfolio (Best Practices)
- **Batching**: Professional managers should schedule property inspections and bill payments on the same day monthly to maximize efficiency.
- **Reserve Fund**: It is recommended to maintain a separate reserve account containing **10% of gross annual rent** to cover emergency repairs and unexpected vacancies.
