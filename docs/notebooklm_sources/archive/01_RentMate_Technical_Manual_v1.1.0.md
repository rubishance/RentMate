# RentMate Technical User Manual

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1.  Download the RentMate app or visit the web portal.
2.  Click **"Sign Up"** and enter your full name, email, and password.
3.  **Note:** You will automatically be placed on the **Free** plan, which supports up to 1 property.

### Adding Your First Property
1.  Go to the **"Properties"** tab.
2.  Click **"+ Add Property"**.
3.  Enter the required details:
    *   **Nickname:** A name to help you remember (e.g., "The Tel Aviv Apartment").
    *   **Address:** Full street address.
    *   **Specs:** Number of rooms and size (sqm).
4.  Click **"Save Property"**.

## üìù Managing Contracts

### Creating a Digital Contract
RentMate allows you to generate a standard rental agreement legally compliant with Israeli law.
1.  Navigate to **"Contracts"**.
2.  Select **"New Contract"**.
3.  Fill in the **Tenant Details** (Name, ID, Phone).
4.  Set the **Lease Terms**:
    *   Start & End Dates.
    *   Monthly Rent Amount.
    *   Payment Day (e.g., "1st of every month").
    *   **Linkage (Hatshmada):** Choose if rent is linked to the Index (Madad) or USD/EUR.
5.  **Review & Sign:** You can sign digitally on the screen. The tenant will receive a link to sign as well.

### Uploading an Existing Contract
If you already have a signed paper contract:
1.  Go to **"Contracts"**.
2.  Choose **"Upload PDF/Image"**.
3.  Our AI will scan the document and extract key dates (Expiration, Options) automatically.

## üí∞ Tracking Finances

### Recording Payments
1.  Go to the **"Dashboard"**.
2.  You will see a list of due payments.
3.  Click **"Mark as Paid"** when you receive money.
4.  You can attach a photo of the transfer confirmation or check.

### Expense Tracking
1.  Go to **"Documents"** > **"Expenses"**.
2.  Upload photos of receipts (Repairs, Plumbers, Renovations).
3.  Categorize them (e.g., "Maintenance").
4.  This helps you calculate your true profit (Net Yield) at the end of the year.
