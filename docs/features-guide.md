# RentMate Features Guide

## Advanced Calculator
The **Calculator** helps you manage rent adjustments and debts.
- **Linkage (Hatzmada):** Automatically calculates rent increases based on the Consumer Price Index (CPI/Madad).
- **Debt Calculation:** Calculates total debt including interest and linkage differentials.
- **Payment Schedule:** Generates a detailed payment schedule for contracts.

## Properties Management
- **Property Details:** Store all info like address, size, floor, and amenities.
- **Documents:** Upload deeds, insurance policies, and maintenance records in the "Documents Hub".
- **Gallery:** Store photos of the property.

## Contracts & Tenants
- **Digital Contracts:** Create legally binding rental agreements.
- **Tenant Profiles:** Manage tenant contact info and history.
- **Renewals:** Get notifications when a contract is about to expire.

## Financials
- **Income Tracking:** Monitor monthly rental income.
- **Expenses:** Track maintenance costs and other expenses (coming soon).
- **Tax Reports:** Generate basic data for your annual tax filing (10% route or full reporting).
